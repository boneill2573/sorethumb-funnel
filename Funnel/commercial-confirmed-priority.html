BEFORE OUR CALL

What to have ready.

A few minutes of prep means we walk in with a plan, not a discovery. Have these handy on the call.

  • Your last 90 days of lead-gen spend — what you've spent across lead lists, ZoomInfo, trade shows, paid social, anything paid.
  • Lead-to-submission conversion rate — roughly, what percentage of leads become submissions? An honest estimate is fine.
  • Top 3 producers' bind rates — ballpark. Helps us model the impact a clean pipeline would have on your top performers.
  • The single biggest bottleneck — if you could fix one thing about your acquisition tomorrow, what would it be?
  • A quiet space with audio — this is a working call, not a sales pitch. We'll be sharing screens and walking through real numbers.
